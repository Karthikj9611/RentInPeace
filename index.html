Frequently asked
How do I post a property for free?
Tap the + button or "Post a Property" in the menu, fill in your property details and owner contact, then submit. Your first listing is free.
Is there a brokerage or fee?
Quatar connects you directly with owners and tenants — no brokerage fees on standard listings.
How are listings verified?
We verify owner contact details and periodically review listings for accuracy. Look for the verified badge on trusted listings.
