Frequently asked
How do I post a property for free?
Tap the + button or "Post a Property" in the menu, fill in your property details and owner contact, then submit. Your first listing is completely free.
How long does my listing stay active?
Listings stay active for 90 days by default. You can renew or edit them anytime from your account. Once a tenant moves in, mark the property as rented so others don't waste time.
Can I edit my listing after posting?
Yes. Go to your profile, find the listing, and tap Edit. Changes go live immediately after review.
Is there a brokerage or fee?
Yes, we charge a one-time brokerage of ₹4,000–₹6,000 from the tenant — split in two parts:

₹2,000 upfront before we shortlist & show you 4 properties matching your requirement.

₹2,000–₹4,000 on the day you like a house and make an advance payment to the owner.

No hidden charges beyond this.
How is the security deposit handled?
The deposit is agreed directly between the owner and tenant. We recommend paying only after physically visiting the property and signing a rental agreement. Never pay in advance to unverified owners.
How do I reset my password?
On the login screen, tap "Forgot password" to receive a reset link.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Menu → Account Settings → Delete Account. This will permanently remove your profile and all listings. This action cannot be undone.
How are listings verified?
We verify owner contact details and periodically review listings for accuracy. Look for the green Verified badge on trusted listings. Always visit the property in person before paying any amount.
How do I report a suspicious listing?
Tap the three-dot menu on any listing and select "Report". Our team reviews all reports within 24 hours. Fraudulent listings are removed immediately.
How do I schedule a property visit?
Open the property, tap "Schedule Visit", pick a date and time slot that works for you. The owner will be notified and will confirm via call or WhatsApp.
Can I reschedule or cancel a visit?
Yes. Go to your scheduled visits and tap Reschedule or Cancel. Please cancel at least 2 hours in advance out of courtesy to the owner.
When do I get my ₹1,000 referral reward?
Your ₹1,000 is credited to your UPI on the day the referred tenant successfully moves into the property via a commission-based booking. There is no limit on how many tenants you can refer.
How does the referral link work?
Share your unique referral link via WhatsApp, SMS, or any app. When the tenant opens it, books a property, and moves in, the referral is automatically tracked to your account.
